## Launch Types

### 1. Open-Close Launch (7-14 Days)

**Best for**: High-ticket products, courses, cohorts

**Pros**:
- Creates urgency and FOMO
- Concentrated effort and energy
- High conversion rates
- Strong momentum

**Cons**:
- Stressful and time-intensive
- Can't buy outside launch window
- Need to rebuild list each time

**Example**:
- Open cart Monday 9am
- Close cart Sunday midnight
- Sell $50K-$200K in one week

### 2. Evergreen Sales (Always Available)

**Best for**: Templates, tools, low-ticket products

**Pros**:
- Passive income
- Buy anytime
- Less pressure
- Compound growth

**Cons**:
- Lower urgency
- Requires consistent traffic
- Slower initial growth

**Example**:
- Always-on sales page
- Automated email sequence
- Steady $5K-$20K/month

### 3. Hybrid Model

**Best for**: Flexibility and maximum revenue

**Strategy**:
- Product always available
- Quarterly launches with bonuses
- Launch gets 50-70% of quarterly revenue
- Evergreen gets 30-50%

**Example**:
```
Quarterly Launch: $75K (3 days)
Evergreen Sales: $25K (87 days)
Total Q1: $100K
```

### 4. Rolling Launch

**Best for**: Membership sites, cohort-based courses

**Strategy**:
- Open cart monthly or quarterly
- New cohort starts every X weeks
- Continuous improvement cycle

**Example**:
- Cohort 1: January (open Dec 15-31)
- Cohort 2: April (open Mar 15-31)
- Cohort 3: July (open Jun 15-30)
- Cohort 4: October (open Sep 15-30)

- 40% Paid ads (multi-platform)

## Common Launch Mistakes

### ‚ùå Launching Too Soon
**Problem**: Product isn't ready, no social proof  
**Fix**: Get 10-20 beta users first, gather testimonials

### ‚ùå No Email List
**Problem**: Launching to crickets  
**Fix**: Build list for 4-8 weeks minimum (goal: 100+ subscribers)

### ‚ùå Weak Sales Page
**Problem**: Traffic but no conversions  
**Fix**: Problem-agitate-solution format + social proof

### ‚ùå Ignoring Post-Launch
**Problem**: Sales drop off immediately  
**Fix**: Nurture buyers, collect testimonials, plan next launch

### ‚ùå One-Time Launch
**Problem**: Leaving 70% of revenue on table  
**Fix**: Re-launch quarterly or transition to evergreen
