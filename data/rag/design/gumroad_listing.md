# What this is

Gumroad is a platform for selling digital products directly to customers. A well-optimized Gumroad listing increases visibility and conversions.

# When to use

- **Selling digital products**: eBooks, planners, templates, courses
- **No website needed**: Gumroad acts as your storefront
- **Creator-friendly**: 10% fee (or 0% with Gumroad+ at $10/mo)
- **Fast setup**: Launch in 15-30 minutes

# 5-step build recipe

1. **Product title**: Clear, benefit-driven (not just "eBook")
2. **Cover image**: Eye-catching, professional (1600x1200px recommended)
3. **Description**: Problem → solution → what's included → CTA
4. **Pricing**: Test $9-$29 for starter products, offer discounts
5. **Preview/sample**: Show 2-3 pages to build trust

# Outline template

```
Title: [Benefit] for [Audience] – [Product Type]
Example: "Meal Prep Mastery for Busy Moms – 30-Day Planner"

Cover image: High-quality mockup or product screenshot

Description:
- Pain point: "Struggling with [problem]?"
- Solution: "This [product] helps you [achieve result]"
- What's included: Bullet list of contents
- Who it's for: Target audience
- Money-back guarantee: 14 days

Pricing: €14.99 (or name-your-price option)

Preview: First 3 pages or sample chapter
```

# Gumroad optimization tips

- **Use tags**: Add relevant keywords (productivity, meal prep, budget)
- **Offer variations**: Basic, Premium, Bundle pricing tiers
- **Upsell**: "Customers also bought..." related products
- **Email collection**: Opt-in for future updates and launches
- **Affiliates**: Enable affiliate program (10-20% commission)

# Pricing strategies

- **Name-your-price**: Minimum €5, suggested €14
- **Limited-time discount**: Launch at 30% off first week
- **Bundle deals**: 3 products for price of 2
- **Tiered pricing**: Basic €9, Premium €19, Complete €29

# Common mistakes

- Generic title ("My eBook" instead of "7-Day Meal Prep for Beginners")
- No preview (customers can't see what they're buying)
- Poor description (vague, no clear benefits)
- Overpriced for first product (€49+ with no social proof)
- No social proof (add testimonials as you get them)

# Metrics to watch

- Views (traffic to your listing)
- Conversion rate (views → sales)
- Average order value (upsells, bundles)
- Refund rate (quality indicator)
