# What this is

A workbook is an interactive eBook with exercises, templates, and worksheets that guide users through a transformation process (e.g., goal setting, business planning, self-discovery).

# When to use

- **High-value offers**: €29-€99 (more interactive than eBooks)
- **Coaching alternative**: DIY version of your coaching program
- **Lead magnet upgrade**: Free mini-workbook → paid full version
- **Course companion**: Supplement to online course or workshop

# 5-step build recipe

1. **Define transformation**: What result will users achieve by completing it?
2. **Outline 10-20 exercises**: Each builds on the previous one
3. **Create templates**: Fillable worksheets, checklists, action plans
4. **Add examples**: Show completed worksheets for clarity
5. **Export as PDF**: Fillable fields or printable with space to write

# Outline template

```
- Title: [Transformation] Workbook
- Introduction: What you'll achieve
- Exercise 1-10: Guided prompts + fillable templates
- Action plans: Step-by-step implementation
- Progress trackers: Milestones and checkpoints
- Resources: Links, recommended tools
- Next steps: CTA or upsell
```

# Pricing

- **Mini-workbook**: €14-€29 (5-10 exercises, lead magnet)
- **Core workbook**: €29-€59 (10-20 exercises, main offer)
- **Premium bundle**: €79-€149 (workbook + templates + bonuses)

# Common mistakes

- Too many exercises (overwhelming, users don't finish)
- No examples (users don't know what "good" looks like)
- Not fillable (requires printing, less convenient)
- No progress tracking (users lose motivation)

# Metrics to watch

- Completion rate (how many finish the workbook)
- Testimonials (transformation stories)
- Upsell conversion (workbook → coaching/course)
- Refund rate (value perception)

# Quick prompts

"Create a workbook for [audience] to achieve [transformation] in [timeframe] with [number] exercises."

Example: "Create a workbook for freelancers to build a €5k/month business in 90 days with 12 exercises."
