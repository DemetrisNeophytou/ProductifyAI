# What this is

An eBook is a digital guide or educational resource, typically 10-50 pages, that provides structured knowledge on a specific topic. It's one of the most popular and accessible digital products for creators.

# When to use

- **Low-risk starter offer**: Perfect for first-time creators to test the market
- **Lead magnet**: Free or low-cost ($0-$7) to build your email list
- **Mid-tier guide**: Core offer ($9-$29) that provides deep value
- **Premium workbook**: High-value ($29-$59) with exercises and templates

# 5-step build recipe

1. **Pick niche pain**: Identify ONE specific problem your audience faces daily
2. **Outline 10-20 chapters**: Break the solution into small, digestible sections
3. **Draft with examples**: Write practical content with real-world examples and actionable worksheets
4. **Visual assets**: Add 8-12 royalty-free images (Pexels/Pixabay) to enhance readability
5. **Export & CTA**: Format as PDF and include next-step call-to-action at the end

# Outline template

```
- Title: [Promise + Target audience]
- Promise: What the reader will achieve
- Table of Contents: Clear navigation
- Chapter 1-10: Each addresses one pain → one quick win
- Resources: Links, templates, bonuses
- Next Step CTA: What to do after finishing
```

# Pricing

- **Lead magnet**: €0-€7 (list building)
- **Core eBook**: €9-€29 (main offer)
- **Premium workbook**: €29-€59 (with templates & exercises)

# Common mistakes

- Topic too generic (e.g., "How to Be Happy" instead of "5-Minute Morning Routine for Busy Parents")
- No CTA at the end (readers finish but don't know what to do next)
- No examples or worksheets (too theoretical, not actionable)
- Poor formatting (walls of text, no visuals)

# Metrics to watch

- Landing page CTR (click-through rate)
- Conversion rate (visitors → buyers)
- Refund rate (should be <5%)
- Completion rate (how many finish reading)

# Quick prompts

"Outline for [audience] to achieve [result] in [time] with [constraints]."

Example: "Outline for busy moms to meal prep 7 dinners in 2 hours with no special equipment."
