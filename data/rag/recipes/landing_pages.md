# What this is

A landing page (or sales page) is a standalone web page designed to convert visitors into buyers or subscribers. It focuses on ONE product or offer with a clear call-to-action.

# When to use

- **Product launches**: Sell your digital product
- **Lead magnet opt-ins**: Collect emails
- **Webinar registrations**: Drive sign-ups
- **Pre-launch waitlist**: Build anticipation

# 5-step build recipe

1. **Hero section**: Headline + subheadline + CTA button (above the fold)
2. **Problem/solution**: Identify pain → position your product as the solution
3. **Features/benefits**: What's included + transformation they'll achieve
4. **Social proof**: Testimonials, reviews, results, trust badges
5. **FAQ + guarantee**: Address objections, offer money-back guarantee

# Outline template

```
1. Hero: Headline + subheadline + CTA
2. Problem: "Are you struggling with..."
3. Solution: "Here's how [product] solves it..."
4. What's included: Bullet list of features
5. Transformation: "After using this, you'll be able to..."
6. Social proof: 3-5 testimonials or case studies
7. Guarantee: 14-day money-back, risk-free
8. FAQs: Address 5-7 common objections
9. Final CTA: "Get instant access now"
```

# Landing page formula

**Hero**: [Desired outcome] in [timeframe] without [common objection]
Example: "Build your first €5k digital product in 30 days without tech skills"

**Features vs. Benefits**:
- Feature: "50-page eBook"
- Benefit: "Finish reading in one afternoon and apply immediately"

# Pricing

Landing pages themselves are free to create, but tools cost:
- **Free**: Carrd, Notion, Google Sites
- **Paid**: Leadpages ($37/mo), Unbounce ($99/mo)
- **Best ROI**: Productify AI built-in templates (included)

# Common mistakes

- Too long (visitors lose interest, scroll fatigue)
- No clear CTA (unclear what to do next)
- Weak headline (doesn't grab attention)
- No social proof (hard to trust)
- No urgency (no reason to buy now)

# Metrics to watch

- Conversion rate (visitors → buyers)
- Bounce rate (visitors who leave immediately)
- Average time on page (engagement)
- CTA click-through rate (interest indicator)

# Quick prompts

"Write a landing page for [product] that helps [audience] achieve [result] in [timeframe]."

Example: "Write a landing page for a productivity planner that helps busy founders save 10 hours per week in 30 days."
